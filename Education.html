Education

Lafayette High School

Lafayette High School is located in Lexington, Kentucky. It is the oldest high school in Lexington, and is home to two magnet programs: School for Creative and Performing Arts (SCAPA) and Pre-engineering program.

Charlie participated in the pre-engineering program which allowed him to take advanced math and science courses. He also took many classes with SCAPA students for his music related classes.

Charlie graduated from Lafayette High School in May 2020 with a 3.9 GPA and recieved academic and music awards.

Governor's Scholar Program

Charlie attended Governor's Scholars Program (GSP) in summer 2019.

GSP is a 5 week summer experience for the highest achieveing incoming high school seniors in Kentucky.

This award also grants a full tuition scholarship to any state university in Kentucky.

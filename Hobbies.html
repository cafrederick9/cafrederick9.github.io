Hobbies

Sports


Charlie is a massive sports fan. His faorite sport is european football. Charlie is a life-long sports fan originating with love for football when he was 4. Charlie regularly plays football and basketball. When not playing sports you can find him watching some sport, mainly football. His favorite team is Manchester United and he has been a loyal supporter who is against the formation of the European Super League.

Charlie also follows many american sports: basketball, soccer, american football, hockey, and baseball. He is a fan of all D.C. sports teams because he was born in the D.C. area. Charlie also is a fan of the University of Kentucky Wildcats because he grew up in Lexington, Kentucky, but his alligence is now with Northern Kentucky Norse.

Choir

Charlie is a professional choral singer who loves all choral music from gregorian chant to post modern. Charlie was a member of Lafayette Madrigals who won their state's chamber competiton both years he was in the choir. Here is a recording of their performance at the Kentucky Music Educators Association Conference in February 2020.

Charlie was also a member in Lexington Christ Church Cathedral's Choir of Men and Boys' since he was 12. Charlie has also taken many other singing gigs outside of his two main choirs in his career. Charlie is currently a member of Northern Kentucky University Chorale and Chamber ensembles.

Video Games

Charlie also plays his fair amount of video games when he gets the opportunity. His favorite video game is EA Sport's Fifa series. In June of 2019 he finished in the top 100, 74th, in the world in Fifa 19. Besides Fifa, he enjoys playing other sports, racing, and first person shooter games.
